Training and Career Development

Training simply refers to the process of acquiring the essential skills required for a certain job. It targets specific goals, for instance understanding a process and operating a certain machine or system. Career development, on the other side, puts emphasis on broader skills, which are applicable in a wide range of situations.This includes decision making, thinking creatively, and managing people. However, many organizations still consider training and career development programs expensive.
